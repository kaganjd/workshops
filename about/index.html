about whatisit.tech

i created whatisit.tech for my graduate thesis at nyu-itp over the course of a semester. i'm slowly collecting additional resources for each workshop here. it's very much a work in progress that i hope will evolve as i and others use these teaching materials in the world.

website dependencies

  • slides: reveal.js
  • emoji favicons: albinekb
  • background images: new york public library digital collections
  • i'm using google analytics to see how many people visit each workshop page and how long they stay
